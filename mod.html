FAQ

Do I need a tablet?
+
No — you can start with a mouse, but a tablet makes drawing smoother and more natural. We recommend a pen display or a pen tablet for pressure sensitivity.
Is Clip Studio Paint required?
+
Courses are built around Clip Studio, but many principles apply to other drawing apps. Free trial of CSP is available from the official site.
How long are the lessons?
+
Most lessons are 10–30 minutes long. Project-based courses span multiple short lessons so you can practice in small daily sessions.
